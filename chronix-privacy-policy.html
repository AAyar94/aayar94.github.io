Privacy Policy

Chronix — Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chronix ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Android application (the "App"). By using Chronix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Chronix is designed to work locally on your device. We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Calendar Data

Chronix requests read-only access to your device calendar (READ_CALENDAR permission) solely to allow you to import events as countdowns. Calendar data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to any external server.

2.2 Notification Data

The App may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming countdowns (POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission). Notification content is generated locally from data you create within the App.

2.3 Device & Usage Data

When you install or use Chronix, we may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device model, operating system version, and screen size
  • App version and crash logs
  • Basic usage analytics (e.g., screens visited, feature interactions)

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve App quality and user experience.

2.4 Data Stored Locally

Your countdown events, preferences, widget configurations, and theme settings are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and internal storage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly enable a sync or backup feature.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Provide core functionality — creating, displaying, and managing countdowns
  • Calendar import — reading calendar events to add as countdowns
  • Reminders — scheduling and delivering local notifications
  • Widgets — displaying countdown data on your home screen
  • App improvement — analyzing anonymized usage data to fix bugs and enhance features
  • Compliance — fulfilling legal obligations where applicable

4. Permissions Summary

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Analytics and optional remote services (if enabled)
READ_CALENDAR Import calendar events as countdowns
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Send countdown reminder notifications
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Re-schedule reminders after device restart

5. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

Chronix does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may use third-party services for limited purposes:

  • Analytics — anonymized usage statistics to improve the App

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. No calendar data or countdown content is shared with third parties.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. Network connections (if used) are encrypted via TLS. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device as long as you have the App installed. You may delete your data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its data from Android Settings. Anonymized analytics data may be retained in aggregated form for analytical purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Chronix is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. Changes to This Policy
