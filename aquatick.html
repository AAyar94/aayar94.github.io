Aquatick

Daily hydration tracker with beverage type charts, reminders, and health articles to build better water habits.

Kotlin Compose MVVM Room Retrofit
All Projects

Overview

Stay hydrated,
every day

Aquatick helps you track your daily fluid intake broken down by drink type, visualized as charts. It calculates your personal daily goal, sends reminders throughout the day, and offers health articles to build lasting hydration habits.

  • Track different beverage types separately with chart visualization (Vico)
  • Personalized daily hydration goal calculation
  • Background reminders via WorkManager
  • Curated health articles fetched via Retrofit
  • Lottie animations for a polished UI experience
  • Firebase analytics & multi-module architecture
