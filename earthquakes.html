Last Earthquakes

Overview

Stay informed
about seismic activity

A real-time app showing the last 100 earthquakes recorded in Turkey. Tap any event to see its epicenter on the map along with depth and magnitude details. You can also view the Turkey seismic risk map.

  • Last 100 earthquakes fetched in real time
  • Sort by magnitude (asc/desc) or chronologically
  • Interactive Google Maps view with epicenter marker
  • Depth, magnitude, and location details per event
  • Turkey seismic risk zone map overlay
