Settowally

Ad-free high-resolution wallpaper app with dark & light mode support and a dedicated tablet-optimized layout.

Kotlin Android Retrofit Room
View on GitHub All Projects

Overview

Beautiful wallpapers,
zero ads

Browse and download free high-resolution wallpapers without any ads or distractions. Save your favourites inside the app for offline access. Settowally fully supports dark and light mode, and features a tablet-optimized dual-pane layout for larger screens.

  • Browse & download free HD wallpapers
  • Save favourites locally with Room database
  • Full dark & light mode support
  • Tablet-optimized dual-pane layout
  • Fast image loading with Coil
